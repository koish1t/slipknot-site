About SlipKnot

SlipKnot is a work in progress 2D platformer that's heavily inspired by other 2D platformers. Most notably the Fancy Pants Adventures series and the Sonic The Hedgehog series.

Hell I mainly used the Sonic physics guide while working on Ty's player controller in the pre-alpha! (Also I totally didn't HEAVILY reference the SA2 site for this site and even took the bg ehe..)
